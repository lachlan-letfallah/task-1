Minecraft Movie Details

A Minecraft Movie follows four misfits pulled into the Overworld, a bizarre, cubic wonderland, where they must master this world and embark on a quest with an expert crafter, Steve (played by Jack Black), to return home and save the Overworld from the Nether.

Sharknado Details

Sharknado is a series of Syfy Channel films that satirically depict a natural disaster where sharks are lifted into the air by tornadoes and unleashed upon unsuspecting land-based populations, often resulting in chaos and carnage.

VelociPastor Details

The VelociPastor is a 2018 action, adventure, and comedy film about a priest who, after losing his parents, inherits the ability to turn into a dinosaur and, with the help of a prostitute, uses his newfound power to fight crime and ninjas.
